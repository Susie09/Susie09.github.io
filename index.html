Biography

I am a final year Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The University of Hong Kong since 2014, under the supervision of Prof. Victor O.K. Li. I received my bachelor degree from Tsinghua University in 2013. I was a visiting student with the Tsinghua Natural Language Processing Group from Nov 2016 to April 2017, worked with Prof. Yang Liu. I was a visiting student with NYU CILVR Group from Oct 2017 to April 2018, worked with Prof. Kyunghyun Cho and Prof. Sam Bowman.

I have interests in several broad research directions, such as natural language processing, deep learning, and reinforcement learning.
